HOW TO READ THIS PLAN

Each article includes:
  • Primary keyword — the main term the page targets
  • Title tag — format: KEYWORD | BENEFIT | The International Schools Guide
  • URL slug — final path after the domain
  • H1 — the on-page headline (can differ slightly from title tag)
  • Meta description — 140–160 characters, written to earn the click
  • Breadcrumbs — full trail from Home
  • Internal links TO — pages this article should link to
  • Internal links FROM — pages that should link to this article
  • External links — credible outbound sources to reference
  • Schema — structured data type(s) to apply
  • Word count — target length
  • Notes — anything editorial or structural worth flagging

PRODUCTION CONVENTIONS (apply to all articles)
Currency: All fees expressed in USD. IDR figures may be shown in parentheses. Exchange rate: IDR 16,000 = $1 USD (February 2026). State the rate and date in the footer of every fees article.
Dates: Use "Originally published: [date]" and "Updated: [date]" (only if revised). No "Fact checked ✓" badge.
Accuracy disclaimer: Required at the bottom of every article. Wording in the Article Production Prompt.
Internal linking: Every article must link to its parent hub page. Cross-reference the Internal links TO and FROM columns before submitting copy.
Schema: Flag schema requirements to the developer at the same time as submitting copy.
Social media quotes: Sourced editorially by the team — not via automated search.
Data sources: Use ISG's own published data in preference to third-party aggregators. Do not cite competitor directories (e.g. International Schools Database) as a source.
