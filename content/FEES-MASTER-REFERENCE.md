## JIS — Jakarta Intercultural School
**Source:** Darren's verified data, 25 February 2026
**Academic Year:** August 2025 – June 2026
**Total Annual Fees include:** Tuition, Capital Fee, and Guarantee Fee

| Year Group | Total Annual (IDR) | Total (USD) |
|---|---|---|
| Early Years 1 & 2 — Half Day (age 3–4) | **291,780,000** | **$17,341** |
| Early Years 1 & 2 — Full Day (age 3–4) | **375,780,000** | **$22,333** |
| Kindergarten (age 5) | **505,280,000** | **$30,030** |
| Elementary, Grades 1–5 (ages 6–10) | **527,780,000** | **$31,367** |
| Middle School, Grades 6–8 (ages 11–13) | **600,430,000** | **$35,685** |
| High School, Grades 9–12 (ages 14–17) | **604,330,000** | **$35,916** |
| JIS Learning Center — Primary | **783,580,000** | **$46,570** |
| JIS Learning Center — Secondary | **856,230,000** | **$50,887** |

**One-time fees:**
- Application Fee: IDR 5,300,000 ($315)
- Technology Fee (new students): IDR 6,647,000 ($395) for Gr 1–5, or IDR 23,210,000 ($1,380) for Gr 6–12
- EAL Fee (one-time): IDR 71,318,000 ($4,239)

**Other annual fees:**
- Bus Fee: IDR 64,500,000–69,500,000 ($3,834–$4,131) — optional

**Key notes:**
- JIS has HALF DAY and FULL DAY options at Early Years. The half-day rate ($17,341) is not comparable to other schools' full-time nursery fees. Use the full-day rate ($22,333) for like-for-like comparisons at age 3.
- JIS EY entry age is 3 (Early Years 1 = Pre-K3).
- Fees are NOT flat — they increase at each level from EY through High School.
- JIS always ranked #1 in any ranking it appears in.

**Fee range for ISG profile:** US$17,341–$35,916 (EY half-day to High School)
